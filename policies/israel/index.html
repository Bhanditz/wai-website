Israel (יִשְׂרָאֵל)

Last Updated: 4 April 2017 – Added Israel laws and policies.

Equal Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, as amended

Official law/policy page

  • English: Equal Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, as amended

Details

  • Date enacted: 1998
  • Responsible Entities (Agencies, Ministries, etc.):
    • Ministry of Justice
    • Commission for Equal Rights of Persons with Disabilities
  • Type: Non-discrimination law
  • Scope: Public sector, Private sector
  • Web only: no
  • WCAG Version Used: WCAG 2.0
  • Relevant Standard:
    • WCAG 2.0
      W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0
  • Relevant Documents:
    • Accessibility Legislation
      Guidance from the Commission on meeting the law. WCAG 2.0 Level AA is required.
    • Equal Rights for People with Disabilities (Service Accessibility Adjustments) Regulations
      Hebrew version of the law.
